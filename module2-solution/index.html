RPG Character Classes

Fighter

A warrior with a mastery of various weapons, who is unafraid to jump directly into the fray. Excellent at keeping the attention of enemies.

Ranger

A skilled tracker who thrives in the wilderness. Able to call beasts to their aid and often skilled with a bow or other ranged weaponry.

Wizard

A master of arcane magic, utilizing their wits and wisdom to conjure reality-bending phenomena. Best kept at a distance, for everyone's safety.
